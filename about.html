What Is Soul on Soles?

Soul on Soles is a shoe restoration and customization company located in Lexington, KY. Whether you need your shoes cleaned, soles de-yellowed, or just a paint job, S.o.S can get it done beyond satisfaction. The company has recently celebrated its one-year anniversary and in that time has garnered business in Kentucky, Philidelphia, Michigan, and Pennsylvania. With business spreading so quickly (and the customer reviews to back upz why), you know it can be trusted.

Community Involvement


Soul on Soles participated in the Life's Journey Co. Community Day Event, which took place the same time as the one year anniversary of S.o.S! James Hooten helped make the event a success alongside other small business owners who believe giving back to the community is just as important as providing an economical service.
